About Us

The core values of us are respect, acceptance, and appreciation of the cultural diversity that exists in the world. We envision a world where everyone is free to have their own convictions and accepts that others have the right to live in peace, regardless of the diversity of their beliefs, identity or way of life.

We recognize that intolerance stems from ignorance and fear of different cultures, nations, or religions. Thus, we provide exchange programs that contribute to the understanding and learning of societies with a different culture, religion, political, or economic system.
